How to Play Shikaku

  • Divide the grid into rectangles (and squares).
  • Each rectangle must contain exactly one number.
  • That number equals the area of the rectangle.
  • Drag from one cell to another to draw a rectangle.
  • Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to remove a rectangle.
  • Fill the entire grid to win!
